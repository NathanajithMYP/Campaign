Hello! I'm Your Member of Youth Parliament.

Dedicated to empowering young voices and driving positive change for our community and the UK.

I believe in a future where every young person is heard, valued, and equipped to shape our democratic landscape. Join me on this journey!

My Key Initiatives

Votes at 16

A monumental achievement! After years of relentless campaigning by @UKYP and countless youth advocates, "Votes for 16" is finally a reality across the entire UK. This is a testament to the power of our collective voice on the big democratic stage.

Comprehensive Political Education

Now that "Votes at 16" is secured, our next big push is for comprehensive political education in secondary schools. It's essential that 16 and 17-year-olds are equipped with the right tools, knowledge, and critical thinking skills to truly engage with our political landscape.

Local Youth Engagement

Working to create more opportunities for young people in our local area to participate in decision-making processes, ensuring their voices are heard on issues that directly affect them.
