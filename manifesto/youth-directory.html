Pillar Strategy Briefing

Youth Club Directory Project

Campaign Progress Track Phase 1 Active (15%)

Status: Collecting local club details and mapping out safe spaces across the area.

This is a 3-step project to find, organize, and share up-to-date information about youth clubs and activities across Sunderland so young people can easily find safe places to go.

The Directory Framework

1

1. Online Form & Information Gathering

An anonymous online form where young people, youth workers, and families can submit details about local clubs. The form uses strict verification rules to block spam and random text.

📊

The live Google Form will display here once you paste your iframe embed code.

2

2. Sorting the Data & Making the Mobile App

Form responses automatically go into a private Google Sheet. From there, the data is split so private contact information stays hidden, while public details sync directly to a mobile-friendly Glide App.

  • Privacy First: Personal names and contacts are kept private and are never shown on the website.
  • Easy Searching: Users can quickly filter clubs by age groups, days of the week, and local postcodes.
3

3. Keeping the Directory Up to Date

To keep the directory useful, the database is regularly checked and updated so old or closed clubs are removed and new ones are added.

  • Information Checks: Regular reviews to make sure club times, locations, and details stay accurate.
  • Community Powered: Working closely with local youth providers to keep the list fresh.
